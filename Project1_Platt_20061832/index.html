Welcome!

I’m a 24-year-old filmmaker based in Western Sydney who is making her way to Hollywood. Dedicated and passionate about all I do, I work to my highest potential in any and every role I work in. I am fast learning and adaptable, working well in high-stress and pressured environments. Social and bubbly, working in groups and collaborative spaces is welcomed and an easy fit. I’ve been enthusiastic about the film industry from a very young age, often making “blockbusters” with my siblings and cousins. I am driven by the surreal and critical aspects of film and its ability to inspire and push change.
